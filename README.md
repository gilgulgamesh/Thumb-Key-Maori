## Installation

1. Download and install Thumb-key for free from [here](https://github.com/dessalines/thumb-key/releases) (click on app-release.apk). It's also on the app store but at $32nzd

2. Go to [copy-this.yaml](https://github.com/gilgulgamesh/Thumb-Key-Maori/blob/main/copy-this.yaml) and press 
this to copy the contents of the file.

<img width="352" height="90" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/686c1717-61fc-45a5-aec8-9eb3c8e520af" />

4. Go to Layouts, and select `deutsch type-split improved`, and unselect the rest.
5. Go to Modify Keys and paste the text you copied

(recommended)

5. To avoid misclicks: Go to Look And Feel, and set `Position` to `center`, and `Key Size` to `78`.

(optional)

6. Look and Feel -> `Color` to `green`
